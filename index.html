Welcome to Social’s documentation!¶

Social is a Python tool for being creepy and taking advantage of the information a person has made public about themselves. It can:

  • Link together people’s social networking profiles.
  • Gather demographic information about a person as it links these profiles.

It has a plugin-based design that makes it easy to extend with new types of social networking sites. I will gladly accept pull requests for new social networking sites at the GitHub repository – see the “contributing” section for more details.
